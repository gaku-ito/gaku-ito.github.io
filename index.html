Gaku Ito, PhD

Political Scientist

Assistant Professor

Graduate School of Humanities and Social Sciences

Hiroshima University

I am an Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Humanities and Social Sciences at Hiroshima University (広島大学, October 2018 - present, tenure track). My area of research is empirical studies of International Relations, with a primary focus on the causes, dynamics, and outcomes of armed conflicts and historical institutions. My methodological interests include archival sources, event data, and causal inference, image analysis, and spatial analysis techniques.

Prior to joining Hiroshima University, I received a PhD in Advanced Social and International Studies from the University of Tokyo and worked as a Research Fellow at the National Institutes for the Humanities (2016 - 2018).
