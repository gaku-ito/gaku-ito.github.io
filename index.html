Gaku Ito, PhD

Political Scientist

Assistant Professor,

Graduate School of Humanities and Social Sciences,

Hiroshima University

I am an Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Humanities and Social Sciences at Hiroshima University (広島大学, October 2018 - present, tenure track).
My area of research is empirical studies of International Relations (IR), with primary focuses on the causes, dynamics, and outcomes of armed conflicts and historical institutions. Substantively, my recent research projects explore the long-run effects of political violence and historical institutions on contemporary armed conflicts and other forms of political and economic outcomes. Methodologically, my research utilizes archival sources, event data, and causal inference, geographic information systems, and image analysis techniques.
