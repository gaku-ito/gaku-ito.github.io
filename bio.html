About Me

Short Bio

  • Gaku Ito (伊藤 岳 in Japanese characters)
  • b. 1988, PhD in Advanced Social and International Studies (University of Tokyo, February 2018)
  • Current Position: Associate Professor, Osaka Metropolitan University (April 2022 - present)
  • Research Interests: Empirical studies of International Relations (IR, 国際関係論).
    • Substantive: Armed conflicts, legacies of historical events and institutions, repression, state and nation building
    • Methodological: Agent-based modeling, applied econometrics, causal inference, geographical image analysis, machine learning, spatial analysis
